New Threats Call For Heightened WordPress Security

Concerned about the security of your WordPress website?

Perhaps you should be. Since December, hackers have infected over 7000 WordPress websites with malicious keylogger scripts that not only collect both your password data, and that of your users, but also discretely utilizes your computer to mine for cryptocurrency.
These viruses are capable of not only ruining the SEO of your site, but also corrupting its core files, and may possibly even result in the suspension of your website.
If you've suddenly received a blacklist warning from Google, noticed strange behavior with your browser, or unusual changes to your WordPress files, you may be infected!
